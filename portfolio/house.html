Hummingbird House

2015
Sheet Metal, Cardboard, Polycarbonate, Rivets, Thread, Paper Fasteners
30 in x 30 in
For this project we were required to create a house for a bird. I was inspired by hummingbirds and decided to make a bird house for them. I know they like flowerd so my intent was to make the house look like a flower. I wanted it to have multiple openings because hummingbirds can fly in any direction.
