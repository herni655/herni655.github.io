Eames Magazine

2016

Using The Eames as an inspiration, I decided to create a gallery walkthrough of their lives. It starts with Charles Eames birth date and ends with a conclusion to their legacy. Part of their history is the importance of how they met, and how they came together to contribute to art history as a whole. That is why I chose a timeline method to represent the information.
