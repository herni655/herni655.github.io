About Me

Irene Hernandez is a designer who is a lover of languages, an avid Netflix watcher, food enthusiast, and a daydreamer. She discovered her passion for design during her teen years of art making. She had to study something creative, and is currently attending Parsons School of Design. Where she studies Communication Design, in hopes of one day becoming an Art Director. She divides her time between Manheim PA, where the Amish are the biggest attraction, and New York City, The City That Never Sleeps.
