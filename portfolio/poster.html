Irene Hernandez
Typography

Mariant Brandt

2016
17 in x 24 in
I was inspired by Mariant Brand and her success at the Bauhaus. Brandt was very unique so I decided to make this poster unique as well. I knew I wanted to have an object in the middle based on one of her works. I combined her major influences, and few pieces of her work to make the object in the middle. I then used typography to say some things about Brandt. I kept it simple because there's a lot going on with the object and the grid.
