Open Source

    Open Source influences education at all levels

    Open Up Resources is a non-profit collaboration of 13 U.S states. It aims to provide a quality standard of open educational resources(OER's).
    This allows great resources for education.

    Since 2010 29,000 students completed the Wiki Ed program. That means they have added 25 Million words to Wikipedia.
    They have contributed to the printing of words in Encyclopedia Britannica.

    Kids On Computers

    Non-profit organization that sets up computer labs around the world where children don't have access to computers and internet.
    Content often includes Wikipedia, educational videos, and games. This allows children to learn in an environment that they
    would not have access to if it wasn't for Kids On Computers.

    OpenWords

    OpenWords aims to be an open source resource for language learning. Lessons are customized to fit the need of an individual.
    It also benefits teacher to customize the content for their students.

    Ikea Open Source Sofa

    Ikea is hosting a competition that encourages third party add-ons. Interesting to see how open source is not just about the internet,
    but can applied to furniture.
